RUC model: an effective model for the local mechanical analysis of submarine power cables

A FORTRAN code and a python code are provided. The former is for the periodical boundary conditions while the latter is for the beam and shell element in pre-processing process.

Paper: Fang, P., Li, X., Jiang, X., Hopman, H. and Bai, Y., 2024. Development of an effective modeling method for the mechanical analysis of three-core submarine power cables under tension. Engineering Structures, 317, p.118632. Link
Fang, P., Li, X., Jiang, X., Hopman, H. and Bai, Y., 2023. Bending study of submarine power cables based on a repeated unit cell model. Engineering Structures, 293, p.116606. Link
