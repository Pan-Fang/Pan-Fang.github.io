RUC model: an effective model for the local mechanical analysis of submarine power cables

A FORTRAN code and a python code are provided. The former is for the periodical boundary conditions while the latter is for the beam and shell element in pre-processing process.

Paper: E. García-Macías, Z.D. Harris, E. Martínez-Pañeda. TDS Simulator: A MATLAB App to model temperature-programmed hydrogen desorption. International Journal of Hydrogen Energy (2024)
